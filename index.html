Welcome to the website of the german band Scorpions! Scorpions are a German rock band formed in 1965 in Hanover by Rudolf Schenker. Since the band's inception, its musical style has ranged from hard rock to heavy metal. This website is where you can view the latest news and tour schedules, listen to the bands new and classic music, and view pictures of the band.
